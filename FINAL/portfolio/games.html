Avast!

Watch the Gameplay Video Here!


Stuff to Know

  • What is it?
    Avast is a bit of a money managing strategy game, where you take your ever growing pirate crew and pillage worlds across the galaxies.
  • What was it for?
    This game was made as part of a Video Game Development course within a 4-5 week time period, with a group, under the theme of Pirates.
  • What did YOU do to contribute?
    I designed the pirate ship icons, which were made to look piraty(?) mixing in anchors, hooks and sails into the design while trying to make it fit the type of ship it's meant to represent.
    I also designed some of the main menu button graphics.
