Comments

  • A lot of my earlier work was very dark(in a literal sense)
  • I used to do quite a few speedpaints to test out lighting on different things.
    Why I decided to test it out on an elaborate demon skeleton creature I'll never know.
  • Some pieces were fanart/commissions of other people's characters (some more obvious that others).
    Strangely enough those usually turned out to be some of my better pieces.
  • I used to draw some pictures/comics for various contests which allowed me to try some new picture ideas (like a video game compilation of a Final Fantasy-esque battle)
  • Some of the art mmmmmmay be a little too violent for some people, but I wanted to try some thing a little more bloody.
    May have gone a little overboard at times.
  • I don't mind people sharing my artwork so long as proper credit is given
