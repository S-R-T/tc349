Welcome to the Fire Pit


Fueled by game art and mild html knowledge.

I'm an artist whose skills come from fleeting lessons taught in school, some experimentation, and a few too many PhotoShop tutorials. A lot of the work I do is inspired by a number of things (games, comics, movies, etc.), but they tend to fall into the more action oriented categories, with some doses of sci-fi, fantasy, supernatural, and (sometimes) prehistoric elements to add some spice. On occasion I draw stuff with an absurd sense of humor to it, like maybe a bear with sharks for arms and cannons for legs or maybe a T-rex with a bulldozer made of chainsaws for a head attacking zombies. If that doesn't give you a sense of what to expect from this little portfolio, then I'm not sure what will.
Regardless, I hope you find some sort of enjoyment out of what you see here.
