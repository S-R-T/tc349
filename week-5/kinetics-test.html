Psychokinetics

Here you will see small decription of various kinds of psychokinetics for reference purposes in terms of writing fiction
Type description
psychokinesis The base of kinetics, the abilities to alter/move/change without having to make contact through physical means.
Telekinesis Essentially like The Force from Star Wars, but more full out with the mind; can move and manipulate objects through mental ability. Can come in two forms: Microkinesis(manipulating small objects) and Macrokinesis(large objects)
Pyrokinesis Manipulation of heat and fire, be it warker an area or throwing fire balls.
Hydrokinesis Much like telekinesis but specifically focused on the manipulation of liquids.
Cryokinesis A sort of subability to Hydrokinesis in that it manipulates liquids to drop down to a freezing point.
Geo/Terrakinesis Manipulation of the earth, in which one literally can move mountains.
Aerokinesis Manipulating the flow and force of the air/wind.
Electrokinesis Manipulation of electrical energies, currents, and generate electricity with the mind.
